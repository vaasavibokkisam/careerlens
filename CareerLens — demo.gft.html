      Frequently Asked Questions

      How do I compress a video online?

      Upload your video, select a target resolution and bitrate, choose the output format, then click "Recompress video!" The lower the resolution and bitrate, the smaller the output file.

      Will compression reduce video quality?

      Yes, compression involves a quality-size tradeoff. Lower bitrate values produce smaller files but with reduced visual quality. For most uses, 720p at 2000-3000 kbps provides a good balance.

      What is the best format for compressed video?

      MP4 (H.264) is recommended for maximum compatibility across devices, browsers, and platforms. WebM is a good alternative for web-only use.

      Is this compressor free?

      Yes, 100% free: no watermarks, no signup, no limits. Files are deleted one hour after upload.
